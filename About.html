BRIEF HISTORY

Palawan, located in the Philippines, ha a rich history.
It was home to indigenous people long before Spanish colonization
In the 15th century, Chinese traders visited the area.
The spanish arrived in the 16th century, establishing settlements and
converting locals to Christianity.
